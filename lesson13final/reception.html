Services: Reception

Have your Wedding Reception in our hall

Each of our Inns include a beautiful hall that can be reserved and decorated for your special day. Choose from our assortment of simple but elegant decorations or bring in your own to make your celebration memorable.

Our experienced staff can take care of the decoration and set-up so that you and your loved ones can relax. Don't worry though, you can choose to assign someone to oversee set-up to make sure your day is perfect.

Enjoy a meal with your guests

Choose your meal from our simple catering menu and have the food prepared according to your schedule, or use our provided kitchens for a special meal prep. Our wait staff will work to make sure your day goes according to your wishes. They will help serve food and clean up following the meal so that your guests can enjoy the reception.
