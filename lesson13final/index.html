Temple Spotlight: Rexburg, ID

The Rexburg Idaho temple was built to accomodate the population of students at BYU-Idaho. The temple was dedicated in 2008 and since then has accomodated numberless students throughout their university experience. Many of the temple workers are students who volunteer their time while also taking classes.

The temple contains two endowment rooms and many sealing rooms. They have a large focus on accepting walk-in patrons throughout the day. Clothing rental is available, however there is no cafeteria in the temple. Feel free to come have lunch or dinner with us after your session!
