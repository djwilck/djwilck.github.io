Snow couldn't stop Octoberfest

by Paige Wilcken

Octoberfest wasnâ€™t stopped by the early snowfall this year. The two-day celebration was a hit with more than 2,000 people in attendance. The booths were fully equipped with candy and propane heaters for the visitors to stay warm and happy. However, the main event was what really drew the crowds in with the performance David Archuleta put on. The concert was a big success especially since the snow had let up just in time for the show to begin. Despite the cold weather everyone managed to stay warm. Although some wanted to cancel the event because of the 1 inch of snow that would come, they did not want to cancel this huge event that only happens once a year in the small town of Soda Springs. This huge event was a great success thanks to David Archuleta and the many vendors that put on a great event.
